Follow-Button-for-FeedBurner by ADITYAJAIN

Adds a floating Follow Button to FeedBurner using Self Hosted WordPress sites. The Same Follow Button which appears on every WordPress.COM Blog.

This plugin collects inserts a Follow Button on the bottom right corner of your website (the follow button which appears on every wordpress.com blog) and uses it to collect email address and passes them to your FeedBurner Email Subscription Database. Thatmeans you can use your existing FeedBurner Account to send email updates to your subscribers. NOTE: This plugin requires FeedBurner Email Subscription Service to be activated If you do not use FeedBurner you can download the "Follow" Plugin instead. Follow Plugin is listed in the WordPress plugin repository at http://wordpress.org/extended/plugins/follow Tweet @TheAdityaJain if you require some tutorial any further help, queries, problems & hacks. Visit http://www.mywp.in for tutorials, tips & tricks and hacks.

Install

1.Install and Activate the "Follow Button For FeedBurner" Plugin 2. Goto WordPress DashBoard>>>Settings>>>Follow for FeedBurner and enter your FeedBurner Feeds ID and Save. To activate Email Subscription Service for your feedburner feed, Login to your FeedBurner account >> Click on the Feed of your website >> Click Publicize >> Email Subscription and click activate.
